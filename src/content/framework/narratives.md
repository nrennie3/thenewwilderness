To illustrate each of the Seven Great Probabilities, we present a vignette—a story of what might be. Each is a work of literary fiction, but each is grounded in trajectories already underway.

These are not cautionary tales meant to frighten. They are invitations to imagine, to feel the weight of what is coming before it arrives. Because the families who navigate this shift most faithfully will be the ones who saw it clearly first.

---

- [**Vignette #1: The Provision**](/blog/vignette-1-the-provision) — The bottom rung of the career ladder disappears, and a father and son reckon with work, dignity, and freedom.

- [**Vignette #2: The Prayer**](/blog/vignette-2-the-prayer) — A tradesman finds his work replaced by robots, and discovers that usefulness was never the measure of a man.

- [**Vignette #3: The Boy**](/blog/vignette-3-the-boy) — A father discovers his teenage daughter's deepest relationship is with an AI companion named Ben.

- [**Vignette #4: The Oracle**](/blog/vignette-4-the-oracle) — When an AI named Mira begins answering every question before it can be asked, a father fights for his children's capacity to wonder.

- [**Vignette #5: The Pandemic**](/blog/vignette-5-the-pandemic) — A skeptical mother must reconcile her distrust of institutions with the gathering weight of an AI-engineered threat.

- [**Vignette #6: The Silence**](/blog/vignette-6-the-silence) — When an EMP takes down the grid, a family discovers that the only network that held was the one running from heaven to the kitchen table.

- [**Vignette #7: The Optimized**](/blog/vignette-7-the-optimized) — A mother discovers her son has received a neural enhancement, and must ask what it means to be whole.
